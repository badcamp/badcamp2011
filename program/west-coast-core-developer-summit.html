October 21 to 23, 2011

UC Berkeley, California

A culmination of the brightest Drupal minds in the technology hub of the world

1500
attending

West Coast Core Developer Summit

$0.00

Friday, October 21st: 9:30am - 7:00pm

International House Auditorium
2299 Piedmont Avenue
Berkeley, CA 94720
(510) 642-9490

Current and future core contributors are invited to submit a lightning presentation on a single problem, or desired feature for improving Drupal core. Lightning talk presentations can be submitted to address one of the three topics:

- Why X sucks and how to improve it.
- Wouldn't it be awesome if the Drupal framework could do X?
- Wouldn't it be awesome if the Drupal product could do X?

Angela Byron will be lead cat-hearder. Dries Buytaert will have the last say. Kieran Lal will act as master of ceremonies. A committee of core contributors will review all lightning talks and decide which should be included.
