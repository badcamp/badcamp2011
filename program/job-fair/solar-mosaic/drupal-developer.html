October 21 to 23, 2011

UC Berkeley, California

A culmination of the brightest Drupal minds in the technology hub of the world

1500
attending

Drupal Developer

Company: 
Solar Mosaic

Solar Mosaic is a platform that anyone can use to create solar projects and finance them from their communities. Solar Mosaic connects people who want to go solar with the buildings that have the ideal roofs and space to go solar. Mosaics are works of art where many pieces come together to form a whole. With Solar Mosaics, many people come together to build community solar projects.

The Position:
Solar Mosaic is looking for a Drupal development superhero with a passion for social change to help us build the worlds first community solar platform.

You will work in a growing team of developers as we constantly improve our online platform, building features that allow communities to come together, build solar projects, track their impact and see their prosperity grow. Working closely with our team members and our community, you will help create a vision for the future of our product. The tools you help build will be used by communities around the world to build prosperity through clean energy. You will be a super hero.

Required Skills/Experience

  • Expert Level Drupal Skills (Module Development, Themeing, Configuration)
  • Agile Project Management (we use Pivotal Tracker)
  • LAMP Stack Management
  • Performance Monitoring and Optimization
  • High Volume E-Commerce Site Management
  • Member Data and Server Security
  • Management of Remote and Overseas Developers
  • Passion for Building a Community Solar Movement

Position is part-time to start with potential for full-time in the near future.

Competitive salary with benefits and earned equity commensurate to experience.

Email your resume and cover letter to theteam@solarmosaic.com with your name and the position in the subject line, like this ‘John Doe for Drupal Developer’

Check out our website to learn more about us.
