October 21 to 23, 2011

UC Berkeley, California

A culmination of the brightest Drupal minds in the technology hub of the world

1500
attending

Client Advisor, Drupal Gardens

Company: 
Acquia

The economy is fueling open-source adoption and Drupal is taking off. If you are interested in getting involved in the next big thing, look at Acquia, the leading provider of commercial support for Drupal. "Home" of Drupal creator Dries Buytaert, we are hiring both full-time and part-time help!
Acquia is a springboard for motivated young professionals to get their foot in the door of one of the hottest tech startups. We are seeking people who are passionate about the web and site development, including cross-browser, cross-platform website building experience. We need smart, focused and determined individuals who have a proven ability to listen, read between the lines, and diagnose and solve problems efficiently.
We’re like the Genius Bar, only with a lot less attitude.
Eager learners desiring to add Drupal to their tech toolbox, step right up to the front of the line and come on board. Recent graduates, career changers, and those coming from non-traditional backgrounds that can hit home runs are encouraged to apply.
Positions are available locally in Boston, Portland OR, and Oxford UK.

Responsibilities:
You will join a dedicated team of remote consultants; helping Drupal Gardens users all over the world build killer web experiences with this amazing SaaS tool. As part of that team, you will work directly with our customers helping them resolve issues and providing coaching on CSS and Drupal. You will develop and maintain excellent relationships with our customers.
You will also have a chance to work with some of the web’s sharpest technical minds, and contribute to making Acquia and Drupal even more awesome. In this entry-level position, you will have an opportunity to learn constantly and will be at the start of an open-ended career at Acquia.

Qualifications:
• Passion for the web, open source, and for helping us build a hot and growing start-up
• Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
• Proficient in HTML, CSS, and other Web 2.0/3.0 technologies
• Strong knowledge of Drupal a plus
• Bachelor's or master's degree in Computer Science or related discipline preferred

Summer 2010 internships also available; please indicate interest with your resume submission.

Submit resume: www.acquia.com/careers
