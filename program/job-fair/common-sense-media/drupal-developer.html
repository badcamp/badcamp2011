October 21 to 23, 2011

UC Berkeley, California

A culmination of the brightest Drupal minds in the technology hub of the world

1500
attending

Drupal Developer

Company: 
Common Sense Media

Common Sense Media is the leading independent, non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of kids and families by providing the information, education, and independent voice they need to thrive in a world of media and technology.

We are currently seeking a talented, experienced Drupal Developers to join a team of 4 Drupal developers, a mobile engineer, product managers, design and QA staff to work on a rapidly expanding Drupal site.

This is a once in a life time opportunity to work on one of the nation's major Drupal consumer sites that reaches over 70 million homes and tens of thousands of schools. Our organization has distribution through Google, Yahoo!, Netflix, Comcast, DirecTV, Time Warner, and many others.

WHAT WE NEED TO SEE ON YOUR RESUME

3+ years developing in Drupal 6/7
Significant experience writing custom modules
Significant experience using Views and Panels
Experience using Subversion and/or Git
3+ years developing in CSS, XHTML, jQuery, and Javascript in a cross browser environment
Proven consumer internet experience working in a Linux/Apache/MySql stack with SQL skills
Features Module experience, nice to have
Sysadmin experience, very nice to have (we are hosted on Acquia Managed Hosting, but have other RightScale/EC2 sites as well)
Experience with Lucene or Solr, nice to have
Drupal community involvement, nice to have

THE X FACTOR

Cooperative & constructive attitude working with product and other teams across the organization
Ability to work with minimal supervision and drive your and others' projects to completion
You are calm, mature, organized and able to work on multiple projects with overlapping deadlines as well as troubleshoot technical and resource issues
You are a skilled communicator and collaborator and are inspired by the Common Sense Media mission
You have a great sense of humor -- even when meeting tough deadlines.

WHAT WE OFFER

A chance to work with talented, passionate professionals
Very competitive Salary and Benefits based on experience
An organization that offers work/lifestyle balance
Competitive benefits including, Medical, Dental, Vision and a matching 401(k) plan
The opportunity to really make a difference in the lives of kids this generation and the next!

Common Sense Media values the diversity of its employees and is an Equal Opportunity Employer.

Qualified candidates only please send your resume as a Word attachment for immediate consideration to jobs@commonsensemedia.org.
