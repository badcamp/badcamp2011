Looking for an exciting career in Developing Drupal? Come visit the Job Fair at BADcamp 2011!

Employers:
Thank you for sponsoring BADcamp. Please have your job descriptions on hand, or post them here now.

Staff your booth during the job fair, and present your company. Tell potential hires about the opportunity, and what it's like to work with Drupal.

Job seekers:
Our sponsors have more openings than what's listed below. Stop by each booth to find out about exciting opportunities with Drupal. Brush off your resume, spruce up your portfolio, or just bring your passion for Drupal.

If you would like to be emailed about more opportunities as they become available, be sure to edit your profile and check the box for "I am interested in exploring Drupal career opportunities".
